Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

SaveFood: Expiry Tracker ("SaveFood", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and username. This information is stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore.

Food Inventory Data: Information about food items you add to the app, including item names, categories, quantities, storage locations, purchase dates, and expiry dates. This data is stored locally on your device using Hive database.

Camera and Photos: When you use our scanning features, the app accesses your camera to photograph receipts, food packages, or fridge contents. Photos taken for scanning are processed and are not stored permanently. Receipt text is extracted on-device using Google ML Kit before being sent to our AI service for analysis.

Subscription Data: If you subscribe to Premium, Apple handles all payment processing. We store your subscription status (active/inactive) and plan type in our database to provide Premium features. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card number, or Apple ID password.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (expiry tracking, recipe suggestions, shopping lists)
  • To process scanned receipts and food images using AI-powered analysis
  • To send expiry notification reminders for your food items
  • To manage your Premium subscription status
  • To improve the app experience and fix technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For user authentication and storing user profile data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered receipt and food image analysis. When you scan a receipt or food photo, the extracted text or image is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts: For barcode product lookup. When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts' public database. Open Food Facts Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device text recognition from receipt photos. This processing happens entirely on your device and no data is sent to Google's servers.
  • Apple App Store: For processing subscription payments. All payment information is handled by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your food inventory data is stored locally on your device. User profile and subscription data is stored in Firebase Firestore with industry-standard security measures. We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your food items at any time within the app. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address below.

6. Children's Privacy

SaveFood is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Notifications

With your permission, we send local push notifications to remind you about food items approaching their expiry dates. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" above.
