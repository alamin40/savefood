Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add food items?

Tap the "+" button on the Pantry screen to add items manually. You can also use the camera icon to scan receipts, barcodes, or photograph your fridge contents for automatic item detection.

How does AI scanning work?

Point your camera at a grocery receipt, food package barcode, or your fridge/cart contents. Our AI analyzes the image and automatically identifies food items with their categories and estimated expiry dates. Free users get 10 AI scans. Premium users get 200 scans.

How do expiry notifications work?

SaveFood sends you reminders 2 days before, 1 day before, and on the day a food item expires. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings for SaveFood.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings > tap your name (Apple ID) > Subscriptions > SaveFood. From there you can change your plan or cancel. You can also manage subscriptions from within the app under Profile > Manage Subscription.

How do I restore my Premium subscription?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, go to Profile > Manage Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will restore your active subscription from the App Store.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your food inventory is stored locally on your device. Receipt text is extracted on-device before analysis. We do not sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at savefoodapp.info@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
